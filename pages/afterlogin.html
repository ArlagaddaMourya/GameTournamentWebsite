About the Tournament

The Cyber Nexus Championship is the premier global competition for professional Cyber Nexus players. This tournament features the top 128 teams from around the world competing for glory and a massive $50,000 prize pool. Players will navigate through group stages and elimination brackets in an intense battle of skill and strategy.

Hosted completely online, this tournament allows teams from any region to participate and showcase their talents on the global stage.

Tournament Rules

  • All participants must be at least 16 years of age
  • Teams must consist of 5 main players and up to 2 substitutes
  • All matches will be played on the latest tournament server
  • Group stage: Best of 3 matches
  • Playoffs: Best of 5 matches
  • Grand Finals: Best of 7 matches
